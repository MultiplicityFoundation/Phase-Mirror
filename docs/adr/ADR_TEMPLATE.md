## Context

**What problem are we solving?**

Describe the issue or challenge that requires a decision. Include:
- What circumstances led to this decision
- What constraints or requirements exist
- What assumptions are we making
- Who is affected by this decision

*Be specific and concrete. Avoid vague statements.*

---

## Decision

**What is our answer?**

State the decision clearly and unambiguously. This should be a declarative statement, not a discussion.

Example: "We will use Apache 2.0 license for all source code."

---

## Rationale

**Why this choice?**

Explain the reasoning behind the decision. Include:
- What benefits does this decision provide
- What trade-offs are we accepting
- How does this align with our mission and values
- What evidence supports this decision

*This section answers: "Why is this the right choice for us?"*
