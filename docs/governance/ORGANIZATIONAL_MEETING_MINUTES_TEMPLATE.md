## APPLICATION FOR EMPLOYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER (EIN)

The Board authorized the Treasurer to apply for an Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the Internal Revenue Service using Form SS-4.

**RESOLVED:** That the Treasurer is authorized and directed to apply for an EIN from the IRS, and to take all actions necessary to complete the application.

---

## APPLICATION FOR 501(c)(3) TAX-EXEMPT STATUS

The Board authorized the filing of Form 1023-EZ (Streamlined Application for Recognition of Exemption Under Section 501(c)(3)) with the IRS.

**RESOLVED:** That the President and Treasurer are authorized and directed to prepare and file Form 1023-EZ with the IRS, together with the required filing fee, and to take all actions necessary to obtain recognition of tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

---

## AUTHORIZATION TO OPEN BANK ACCOUNT

The Board authorized the opening of a bank account in the name of the Corporation.

**RESOLVED:** That the Treasurer is authorized to open a checking account with [Bank Name] in the name of Citizen Gardens Foundation, and to execute all documents required by the bank for this purpose.

**FURTHER RESOLVED:** That [Name(s)] are authorized signatories on all bank accounts, and that the bank is authorized to honor checks, drafts, and other instructions signed by such persons.

---

## TRADEMARK APPLICATIONS

The Board authorized the filing of Intent-to-Use (ITU) trademark applications for the following marks:
- QAGI (Query-Augmented General Intelligence)
- Multiplicity
- Phase Mirror
- Citizen Gardens Foundation (word mark and logo)

**RESOLVED:** That the President is authorized to engage trademark counsel to prepare and file Intent-to-Use trademark applications with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) for the marks listed above, and to execute all documents and take all actions necessary for such filings.

**FURTHER RESOLVED:** That the estimated budget for trademark filings (including government fees and legal fees) shall not exceed $5,000 without further Board approval.

---

## SUCCESSOR DESIGNATION (ARTICLE VIII COMPLIANCE)

The President/Lead Architect presented a Successor Designation Instrument in compliance with Article VIII of the Bylaws.

**RESOLVED:** That the Successor Designation Instrument designating [Designated Successor Name] as the successor to the Lead Architect is hereby approved and shall be filed with the Secretary as part of the permanent records of the Corporation.

**FURTHER RESOLVED:** That the Lead Architect shall conduct annual succession planning reviews and update the Successor Designation as needed.
