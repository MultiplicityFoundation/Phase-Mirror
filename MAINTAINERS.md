## Becoming a Maintainer

To become a maintainer, you must:

1. Make 10+ meaningful contributions (PRs, issue reports, reviews)
2. Demonstrate understanding of the protocol and codebase
3. Be nominated by an existing maintainer
4. Be approved by the Lead Architect

### Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review PRs within 7 days
- Triage issues (label, assign, close)
- Merge approved PRs
- Participate in MIP votes
- Uphold the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Follow the [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md)

### Maintainer Privileges

- Push to main branch (via PR only)
- Merge PRs
- Close duplicate/spam issues
- Label and assign issues
